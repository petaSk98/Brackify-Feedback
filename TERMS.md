# Brackify Terms and Conditions

Brackify is a Reddit tournament bracket application designed to run community voting tournaments within selected subreddits.

The application is currently used only within developer-managed communities and for community-driven events.

Brackify allows users to vote in head-to-head matchups until a final winner is determined. Voting interactions occur entirely within Reddit.

The application may be updated, modified, or removed at any time without prior notice.

Users interacting with Brackify remain subject to Reddit’s platform rules and policies.
